1. Who are we?

We are HEC's Internation Society! Our main goal is to jump start your year at HEC may it be by helping you with all the legal and administrative tasks as well as making sure you have fun on campus. As soon as you arrive on campus, we will be hosting a plethora of events that you are free to attend.These will range from campus tours, to welcome drinks, as well as a few Ikea trips to make sure that you get what you need for your stay here at HEC.

2. Our mascot: Benjamin Ternational

Here at HEC, most of the communication is done via emails and Facebook. In order to get the most out of your school year we highly suggest that you follow our icon Benjamin Ternational on Facebook and your promotion's Facebook group!
